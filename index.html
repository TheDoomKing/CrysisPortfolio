Arman Ossi Loko

My name is Arman Ossi Loko and I am a 2D/3D Artist & Web Developer from Bosnia and Herzegovina. Game and Web Development are my passion and I have spent the past 6+ years modding tons of different games, creating various assets for each one of them. Those include GUI changes, playable game maps / levels and 23/3D work, all of which was created 100% from scratch.

Currently studying at Faculty of Information Technology in Mostar, most of my time is spent criticizing movies and video games, writing reviews and modding games on a known game modding community named GameBanana. Got any questions or something I can help with? You can hit me up via the contact form on the Contact page.
